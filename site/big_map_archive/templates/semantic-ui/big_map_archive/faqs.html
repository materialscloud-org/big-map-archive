FAQs

Can I change community once my record is shared ?

  • No, you cannot change the community once you shared the record.
    If you need to change the community please contact us at big-map-archive@materialscloud.org.

How can I give access to a colleague to edit my record ?

  • Click the "Share with link" button and create a link with access "Can edit".
    Copy the link and send it to your colleague.
